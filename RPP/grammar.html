Like Portuguese, English has subject+verb+object


The subjects are:

English Portuguese
I Eu
You Tu/Você(s)/Vós
He/She/It Ele(a)
We Nós
They Eles(as)

Most verbs are conjugated like this in the simple present:

Subject Verb (to do)
I do
You do
He/She/It does
We do
They do

The verb "to be" has a different conjugation:

Subject Verb (to go) Verb (to be)
I go am
You go are
He/She/It goes is
We go are
They go are
